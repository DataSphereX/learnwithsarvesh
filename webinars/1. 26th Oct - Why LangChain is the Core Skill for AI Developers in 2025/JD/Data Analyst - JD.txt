Job Title: Data Analyst
Location: [On-site / Hybrid / Remote – City, Country]
Department: Data & Analytics / Business Intelligence
Employment Type: Full-time
About the Role

We are seeking a detail-oriented and analytical Data Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will transform raw data into actionable insights that drive strategic decisions. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to collect, process, and analyze data from various sources, helping the organization make data-driven decisions.

Key Responsibilities

Collect, clean, and validate data from multiple internal and external sources.

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends, patterns, and correlations.

Develop dashboards and visual reports using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or Excel.

Generate business insights and communicate findings to stakeholders through clear visualizations and presentations.

Collaborate with product, marketing, finance, and operations teams to define data requirements and key performance indicators (KPIs).

Automate recurring reports and optimize data workflows using Python, SQL, or R.

Support A/B testing, predictive modeling, and ad hoc data requests.

Maintain data integrity, ensure data governance, and document data processes.

Required Skills & Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Economics, or a related field.

1–3 years of experience as a Data Analyst, Business Analyst, or in a similar role.

Strong knowledge of SQL for querying databases.

Proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) or R for data manipulation and analysis.

Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker).

Solid understanding of data cleaning, transformation, and reporting techniques.

Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.

Excellent communication skills — ability to explain data insights to non-technical stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications (Nice to Have)

Experience with statistical modeling, machine learning basics, or predictive analytics.

Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Google BigQuery, or Azure Data Studio).

Knowledge of ETL tools and data warehousing concepts.

Exposure to business domains such as finance, marketing analytics, or operations.

What We Offer

Competitive salary and performance bonuses.

Opportunity to work with modern data tools and technologies.

Collaborative environment focused on learning and innovation.

Flexible work arrangements and career growth opportunities.

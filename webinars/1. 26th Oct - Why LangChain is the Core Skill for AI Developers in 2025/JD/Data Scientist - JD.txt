Job Title: Data Scientist
Location: [On-site / Hybrid / Remote – City, Country]
Department: Data & AI / Analytics
Employment Type: Full-time
About the Role

We are seeking an innovative and analytical Data Scientist to join our growing data team. You will be responsible for designing and implementing advanced analytics, machine learning models, and data-driven strategies to support decision-making across the organization. The ideal candidate combines strong statistical and programming skills with a passion for solving real-world business problems using data.

Key Responsibilities

Collect, clean, and analyze large structured and unstructured datasets from multiple sources.

Build, train, and deploy machine learning and predictive models to support key business decisions.

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and uncover actionable insights.

Develop data pipelines and automate model workflows using Python and cloud services.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams (engineering, product, and marketing) to define data requirements and KPIs.

Communicate technical results to non-technical stakeholders through clear visualizations and reports.

Stay current with the latest developments in data science, AI, and analytics tools.

Required Skills & Qualifications

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.

3–5 years of hands-on experience as a Data Scientist or in a similar role.

Strong proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch).

Experience with SQL and database management systems.

Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms, feature engineering, and model evaluation.

Familiarity with data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau, or Matplotlib/Seaborn).

Working knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).

Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications (Nice to Have)

Experience with deep learning or natural language processing (NLP).

Knowledge of big data tools such as Spark or Databricks.

Exposure to MLOps concepts and model deployment in production.

Contributions to open-source projects or published research papers.

What We Offer

Competitive compensation and performance-based bonuses.

Opportunity to work on real-world AI and machine learning projects.

Flexible work options and collaborative team environment.

Learning budget for certifications, conferences, and research.
